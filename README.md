### About me

The years I spent building full-stack applications in production environments clarified something quickly: reliable software is mostly invisible. React, TypeScript, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Python — deployed in cloud-native environments where performance and maintainability are non-negotiable.

What changed the trajectory of my work was going deep on AI — not surface-level prompt engineering, but genuinely understanding how large language models reason, where they fail, and how to architect systems around them responsibly. I hold credentials across Anthropic's practitioner curriculum including Claude Code, AI Capabilities and Limitations, the AI Fluency Framework, and MCP (Model Context Protocol), and I'm currently completing the Claude Certified Practitioner designation. I build AI-powered features into production applications rather than treating them as bolt-ons.

My background includes three years as a Software Engineer at Connect Smart Consulting, owning end-to-end delivery of full-stack client applications. I'm based in Ottawa and currently exploring full-stack roles at startups, consulting firms, remote-first teams, and Government of Canada CS-level positions.
